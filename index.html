Lord Darth Vader

The evil Sith Lord who saved the universe

Lord Vader is seen here asking Luke Skywalker to join him so they can rule the galaxy forever.

Darth Vader has appeared in these famous films:

  • May 25, 1977 - Star Wars: Episode IV – A New Hope
  • May 17, 1980 - Star Wars: Episode V – The Empire Strikes Back
  • May 25, 1983 - Star Wars: Episode VI – Return of the Jedi
  • May 16, 1999 - Star Wars: Episode I – The Phantom Menace
  • May 16, 2002 - Star Wars: Episode II – Attack of the Clones
  • May 19, 2005 - Star Wars: Episode III – Revenge of the Sith
  • December 18, 2015 - Star Wars: Episode VII – The Force Awakens
  • December 16, 2016 - Rogue One: A Star Wars Story
  • December 15, 2017 - Star Wars: Episode VIII – The Last Jedi
